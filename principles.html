Project Principles

A short overview of what BeAwarely stands for.

  • Clarity over hype: useful features first, marketing later.
  • Respect users: no dark patterns, no tricks.
  • Safety matters: moderation exists to keep the space usable.
  • Learning mindset: iterate, test, improve.
  • Demo reality: this is a demo — things can break or change.
